## Technical Stack

### Frameworks & Libraries
- **TensorFlow/Keras** - Deep learning framework
- **OpenCV & Pillow** - Image processing
- **NumPy & Pandas** - Data manipulation
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn** - Visualization
- **Scikit-learn** - ML utilities

### Pre-trained Models
- **MobileNetV2** (ImageNet weights) - Google Research
- Transfer learning base for plant disease classification

### Datasets
- **PlantVillage Dataset** - Available on Kaggle
  - Citation: Hughes, D. P., & Salathé, M. (2015)
  - 54,000+ images of healthy and diseased plant leaves
  - 13 disease classes (10 tomato, 3 potato)
